Overview

Our primary goal for this project was to create a physical prototype to solve an everyday problem with at least one output and input device. In pursuit of this objective, we designed a smart system that aids in optimizing plant growth conditions. The system incorporates sensors to measure crucial environmental factors such as temperature , humidity, and light intensity. By integrating these inputs, the system provides feedback to users through a combination of sensory outputs, specifically a buzzing sound, indicating the ideal location for placing a plant. This smart innovative system alleviates the common daily challenge people encounter when aiming to ensure optimal and healthy growth of their plants. This system stands out due to its ability to dynamically monitor key environmental factors in real-time, providing timely feedback to guide users in identifying the perfect spot for their plants.

System Logic

The smart system aims to assist users in determining the perfect spot for their plants to flourish. Initially, users are required to research and set the necessary conditions that foster the optimal growth for their specific plant species. The system then tracks temperature, humidity, and light intensity until it identifies the ideal environment. Once the ideal temperature, humidity, and light intensity are detected, the buzzer is triggered to alert the user to halt plant movement.

Key Components:

1. DHT sensor: Measures temperature and humidity levels in the environment.

2. LDR (Light Dependent Resistor) / Photoresistor: Measures light intensity.

3. Buzzer: Acts as an output device to signal the user when optimal plant growth conditions are detected.

4. Arduino microcontroller: Facilitates the integration of sensor data and controls the feedback mechanism.

What makes our system special?

Unlike conventional methods that rely solely on static guidelines, this system dynamically assesses the immediate surroundings in real-time, ensuring a precise evaluation of the plant's habitat. What sets this system apart is its user-centric design. It empowers individuals by not only offering data-driven insights but also by guiding them through a sensory feedback mechanism. By emitting a distinctive buzzing sound when optimal conditions are detected, the system creates an interactive and intuitive experience for users. This auditory cue signifies the opportune moment to place the plant, eliminating the guesswork and allowing for a seamless and informed decision-making process.

In essence, the integration of real-time monitoring, personalized guidance, and user interaction elevates this system beyond a mere monitoring tool. It transforms plant care into an engaging and informed experience, offering an innovative solution that simplifies the complexities of nurturing plants while promoting their healthy growth and well-being.
